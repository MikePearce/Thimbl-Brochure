Thimbl is a Manifesto for the Open Web written in code.

The most significant challenge the open web will need to overcome is not technical, it is political.

Welcome to thimbl, the free, open source, distributed micro-blogging platform. If you're tired of being locked in to one micro-blogging platform, or a single social network. Or you're weary of corporations hi-jacking your updates in the pursuit of money, then thimbl is for you.

Thimbl is currently not ready for the world! We're still putting the finishing touches on what we hope will be a useful and successful application.

In the meantime, read more about it or find out how you can help or simply find out what we're up to on the social networks you'd love to get away from.

We've been nominated! - Sep 25 2010

Huzzah! We've been officially nominated as one of the three projects being entered for the Transmediale Open Web award!

This is excellent news as it's been one of the goals of Thimbl as a means to spread the word since it's inception. Thanks to everyone on the team who helped make this happen. Now we need to finish the application and make sure everyone knows about it!
